This article guides you through setting up the connection."

## Step-by-Step Guide

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Connections">
    1. Log in to your Ravical Dashboard
    2. Click on **Connections** in the left sidebar
    3. Locate the **AccountView** tile in the integrations list
    4. Click **Connect** to begin the setup process

    <img
      src="/images/Accountview.png"
      alt="Accountview Pn"
      title="Accountview Pn"
      style={{ width:"75%" }}
    />
  </Step>
  <Step title="Authenticate with AccountView">
    You will be forwarded to AccountView where you need to authenticate using **administrator credentials**. This grants Ravical access to retrieve relevant information from your AccountView environment.

    Once authenticated, the connection is established and Ravical can start accessing information from AccountView.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Check>
  The integration is now successfully added and will be automatically used by Ravical AI agents when needed.
</Check>
