### Step-by-Step Guide

<Steps>
  <Step title="Find Your CRM Group Number">
    Before connecting Alure, you'll need your **CRM Group Number**. This identifier allows Ravical to access the correct Alure environment for your organization.

    <Tip>
      **Where to find it:**

      In most cases, the **CRM Group Number is** **1**, unless specified otherwise by your Alure POC during onboarding. If you're unsure, please contact your Alure administrator or Wolters Kluwer support.
    </Tip>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Navigate to Connections">
    1. Log in to your Ravical Dashboard
    2. Click on **Connections** in the left sidebar
    3. Locate the **Alure** tile in the integrations list
    4. Click **Connect** to begin the setup process

    <img
      src="/images/Screenshot2025-11-19at14.50.29.png"
      alt="Screenshot2025 11 19at14 50 29 Pn"
      title="Screenshot2025 11 19at14 50 29 Pn"
      style={{ width:"57%" }}
    />
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect with CRM Group Number">
    1. Enter your **CRM Group Number** in the input field (typically ‘1’)
    2. Click **Connect**
    3. You will be redirected to Alure where you have to **authenticate using your credentials**
    4. The connection will now be established and Ravical will start accessing relevant information from Alure
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Check>
  The integration is now successfully added and will be automatically used by Ravical AI agents when needed.
</Check>
