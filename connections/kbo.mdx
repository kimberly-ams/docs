---
title: "Kruispuntbank van Ondernemingen (KBO)"
description: "Kruispuntbank van Ondernemingen (KBO) is the official Belgian government database that registers and manages essential information about all businesses and enterprises operating in Belgium. By connecting KBO to Ravical, the Inbox Agent can access company registration details, legal information, and establishment data to draft more accurate and contextual email responses. This article guides you through setting up the connection."
---

## Step-by-Step Guide

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Connections">
    1. Log in to your Ravical Dashboard
    2. Click on **Connections** in the left sidebar
    3. Locate the **Kruispuntbank van Ondernemingen (KBO)** tile in the integrations list
    4. Click **Connect** to connect the KBO as a source of truth.
  </Step>
</Steps>
