Connecting your tools in Ravical enables seamless data access and improves the agent's ability to create a theoretically and practically correct answer to your questions or emails. This guide walks you through the requirements and steps to establish connections in your Ravical dashboard.

### Prerequisites

Before you can connect a tool to Ravical, ensure you meet these requirements:

- **Admin Access to the Tool You Want to Connect:** You must have administrator permissions for the tool you're connecting to Ravical. Without admin access, you won't be able to authorize the connection or grant Ravical the necessary permissions to access your data.
- **Access to the Ravical Dashboard:** You need an active account with access to your organization's Ravical dashboard. If you don't have access yet, reach out to your Customer Value Manager, who can provision your account and provide you with login credentials.

### How to Connect a Tool

Once you've confirmed you have the necessary prerequisites, follow these steps:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in to the Ravical Dashboard">
    Navigate to your organization's Ravical dashboard and sign in with your credentials.

    https://dashboard.prod.ravical.com/
  </Step>
  <Step title="Go to the Connections Section">
    Look for the "Connections" option in the main navigation menu and click on it.

    ![Screenshot2025 11 14at15 11 19 Pn](/images/Screenshot2025-11-14at15.11.19.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Your Connection">
    Browse the available connections and locate the tool you want to connect. Initiate the setup process by clicking the **'Connect'** button below the tool.

    <Tip>
      **Want full details on how to connect a specific tool?** \
      Read the dedicated article in the left-hand column underneath 'Connection: Tools'. 
    </Tip>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Follow the Authorization Flow">
    You'll be redirected to authenticate and authorize Ravical to access your tool. Complete the authorization process as prompted.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Need Help?

If you encounter any issues during the connection process, contact your Customer Value Manager for assistance.
