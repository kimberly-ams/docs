## Step-by-Step Guide

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Connections">
    1. Log in to your Ravical Dashboard
    2. Click on **Connections** in the left sidebar
    3. Locate the **TaxWin** tile in the integrations list
    4. Click **Connect** to begin the setup process

    <img
      src="/images/Taxwin.png"
      alt="Taxwin Pn"
      title="Taxwin Pn"
      style={{ width:"63%" }}
    />
  </Step>
  <Step title="Authenticate TaxWin">
    - Select whether you want to use **GenIA-L** from TaxWin to have Ravical communicate Agent-to-Agent.
    - Fill in the Username and Password of **administrator credentials**. This grants Ravical access to retrieve relevant information from your TaxWin environment.
    - Using **Two-Factor Authentication** for TaxWin? Enter the TOTP secret from your authenticator app.

    <img
      src="/images/Taxwin2.png"
      alt="Taxwin2 Pn"
      title="Taxwin2 Pn"
      style={{ width:"73%" }}
    />
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Check>
  The integration is now successfully added and will be automatically used by Ravical AI agents when needed.
</Check>
