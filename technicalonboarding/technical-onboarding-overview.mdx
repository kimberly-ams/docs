### Part 1: Microsoft 365 & Outlook Add-in

<Steps>
  <Step title="Connect Microsoft 365">
    You've been granted access to the Ravical Dashboard and should have received an email notification about this.

    To set up the Microsoft 365 connection:

    1. Log in to your Ravical Dashboard
    2. Click on **Onboarding** in the bottom left corner
    3. Follow the wizard, which will guide you through the connection steps

    For detailed instructions, see: https://ravical.mintlify.app/TechnicalOnboarding/microsoft-365-email
  </Step>
  <Step title="Install the Outlook Add-in">
    The Outlook Add-in allows your team to access Ravical directly from their inbox.

    For complete installation instructions, see: https://ravical.mintlify.app/TechnicalOnboarding/outlook-addin

    **Important notes:**

    - In step 3 of the installation process, you'll need to add users who should have access to the Add-in
    - Microsoft may take up to 72 hours to make the Add-in visible to users after installation
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Part 2: Set up Integrations

Connect your business tools to Ravical to centralize your data and workflows.

**How to connect integrations:**

1. Navigate to **Connections** in your Ravical Dashboard
2. Click on the relevant sources you want to connect (such as Outlook, Yuki, Silverfin, AdminPulse, or SharePoint)
3. Follow the on-screen steps for each integration
