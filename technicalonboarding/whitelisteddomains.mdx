### **When to do this**

Complete whitelisting during the initial technical setup, before testing begins. This ensures a smooth experience from day one, with no interruptions or performance issues.

### Required URLs

These URLs must be whitelisted for Ravical to function:

- `https://api.prod.ravical.com/`
- `https://outlook-add-in.prod.ravical.com/`
- `https://dashboard.prod.ravical.com/`
- `https://api.ravical.com/`
- `https://outlook-add-in.ravical.com/`
- `https://dashboard.ravical.com/`
