</Info>

### Prerequisites

Before proceeding, ensure you have:

- Microsoft 365 with SharePoint Online
- Global Administrator access to your Microsoft 365 tenant

### Step-by-Step Deployment Instructions

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Microsoft Admin Center">
    Navigate to Add-Ins:

    ```
    https://admin.microsoft.com/Adminportal/#/Settings/AddIns
    ```

    Click **Deploy Add-In** → **Next**

    ![Screenshot2025 11 13at09 36 03 Pn](/images/Screenshot2025-11-13at09.36.03.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Upload the Manifest File">
    - Under **Deploy a custom add-in**, select **Upload custom apps**
    - Choose: **I have a URL for the manifest file**
    - Paste this URL:

    ```
    https://outlook-add-in.prod.ravical.com/manifest.prod.xml
    ```

    - Click **Upload**

    ![Screenshot2025 11 13at09 36 43 Pn](/images/Screenshot2025-11-13at09.36.43.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Assign Users or Groups">
    - Choose who will have access to the add-in (specific groups, departments, or your entire organization)
    - Click **Next**

    ![Screenshot2025 11 13at09 37 53 Pn](/images/Screenshot2025-11-13at09.37.53.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review Permissions & Finish">
    - Carefully review the permissions the add-in requests. These permissions allow the add-in to interact with the user's mailbox and are required for full functionality.
    - Click **Save**

    <Info>
      **Note:** It may take 24–72 hours for the add-in to fully propagate.
    </Info>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Grant Admin Consent">
    This final step ensures the add-in is authorized to access required mailbox data.

    - Visit this URL:

    ```
    https://login.microsoftonline.com/organizations/adminconsent?client_id=053b5d31-fbbd-43dc-a7b5-d22b701b5a08
    ```

    - Review the requested permissions
    - Click **Accept**

    ![Screenshot2025 11 13at09 38 51 Pn](/images/Screenshot2025-11-13at09.38.51.png)
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Troubleshooting & Support

If users don't see the add-in after 72 hours, verify that:

- The add-in was assigned to the correct users or groups
- Admin consent was successfully granted
- Users have restarted Outlook or refreshed their browser
