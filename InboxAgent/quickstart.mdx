---
title: "Getting Started"
description: "Start using the Inbox Agent in 5 minutes"
---

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Outlook & Pick an Email">
    Open your **Outlook inbox** and select any incoming email from a client. The Inbox Agent is designed to work where you already are — directly inside Outlook.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Launch the Inbox Agent">
    Find the Ravical icon in the **Outlook menu**. Depending on your Outlook version, the Ravical icon might look different from the image below.

    ![Screenshot2025 10 21at12 55 29 Pn](/images/Screenshot2025-10-21at12.55.29.png)

    💡 **Can't find the icon**

    - Go to **Apps** in Outlook
    - Search for **Ravical**

    ![Screenshot2025 09 25at10 59 37 Pn](/images/Screenshot2025-09-25at10.59.37.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pin the Inbox Agent">
    Upon clicking the Ravical icon, the Inbox Agent will open as a right-side panel.

    <Info>
      **TIP**: 📌 **Pin the sidebar** so it stays open while you switch emails
    </Info>
    ![Gif Pin Gi](/images/Gif_pin.gif)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review & Refine the Draft">
    The Inbox Agent will generate a **draft reply** based on:

    - The email thread
    - Relevant data from internal tools
    - Your tone of voice

    You can then:

    - **Edit** the draft
    - Ask for changes (e.g., “maak het korter”, “schrijf formeler”)
    - **Add details** the agent missed

    <Tip>
      **TIP:** 📝 **Instruct the agent** to personalise replies:

      > “Gebruik een opsomming”
      >
      > “Voeg een uitleg toe in eenvoudige taal”
      >
      > “Gebruik de standaardafsluiting van het kantoor”
    </Tip>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Use and Send">
    Happy with the result?

    Click **“Use this draft”** to insert the draft reply into your email window, then press send.

      className="mx-auto"
      style={{ width:"31%" }}
    />
  </Step>
</Steps>
